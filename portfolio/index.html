About Me

Hi, I'm Ayden. My passion for computers began at age 8 when I discovered Bitsbox on my mom's old computer. This early exposure sparked my curiosity, leading me to research coding concepts on my school computer whenever possible.

By 14, I had officially started learning Python, which marked the beginning of my serious coding journey. After two years of Python development, I became curious about the inner workings of games and took up C++, a more complex language that allowed me to explore software at a deeper level.

Surprisingly, mastering C++ made Python even more intuitive for me. Now I work comfortably with algorithms in both languages, leveraging each for its unique strengths. Before pursuing college, I'm looking to secure a position in the tech industry where I can help companies grow their brand and software solutions, potentially building a career path that bypasses traditional education routes.
